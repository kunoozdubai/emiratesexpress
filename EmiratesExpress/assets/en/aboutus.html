About Us

Established in 2006, Emirates Express Documents Clearing Company was the first accredited national company in the field of tracking and clearing documents, operating under the certified annual contracts system and providing “Public Relations Officer” service through a dedicated team of Emirati PROs.

Emirates Express Documents Clearing Company is member of Emirates Express Group-Dubai which comprises a number of companies that aim to stimulate and activate a diversified economy in the United Arab Emirates and to render integrated services which enhance the efficiency of corporate performance and success.

Mission

To earn our clients’ trust and total satisfaction
  • Providing integrated services for businessmen;
  • Saving employees and customers’ time;
  • Punctuality, accuracy and integration in the tracking and clearance of documents; and
  • Qualifying young national cadres of UAE and GCC PROs.

Vision

To support our clients’ growth with the best professional services
